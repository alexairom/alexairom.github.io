Here are my favorite TV shows!

One Tree Hill

One Tree Hill takes place in the the fictional town of Tree Hill in North Carolina. It revolves around two half-brothers and the lives they live both in and out of high school.

The brother's names are Lucas and Nathan Scott. The first four seasons show their lives and romances in high school. The last five seasons show a continuation of their lives after college. Viewers get a glimpse into their friends and family in the latter seasons.

The Vampire Diaries

The Vampire Diaries also revolves around the lives of two vampire brothers. Their names are Stefan and Damon Salvatore.

The show consists of eight seasons. One large theme throughout the show is that it revolves around the fictional creatures of vampires and werewolves.
